Japnit Sethi

🌞 A roboticist, an aspiring product manager, and an international student advocate who believes in an empathetic, inclusive & diverse form of leadership.

🌎 On a Technical note, I want to use knowledge from my BSc. Mechanical engineering & MSc. In Computer Engineering with a focus on robotics, controls & autonomy to tap into the consumer market of developing countries and produce highly efficient robots that can help build organic relations between the developed & developing nations.

🤖 Seeing fellow international students struggle to find inspiration to succeed in the North American tech industry, led me to build this platform of “The International Voice”. Via this platform, I bring raw unedited conversations with some of the brightest International tech minds of our generation in a video podcast format, focused on their entire journey to success.

🗽 Let’s build an inclusive, diverse, empathetic & passionate America together.
